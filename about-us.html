About FUN FEST

FUN FEST CALABAR is an annual music art & urban festival that takes place in calabar cross river state Nigeria. The festival plays host to the world and welcomes visitors to sample its culture & seasonal euphoria while making memories that'll last a life time.

Over the year, it has grown to become bigger & better with increasing numbers of people flooding to the states capital to witness and participate in its thrill, fun & Excitement.
